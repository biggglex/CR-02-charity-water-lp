Our Story

The Weight of Water

Clean water changes everything — but only if we make it possible.

Across the globe, millions of people walk hours each day just to collect dirty water — a crisis that steals time, health, and opportunity, especially from women and children. Since 2006, we’ve been working to change that by bringing clean, safe water to communities in need. Every project is tracked, every dollar accounted for, and every life impacted starts with someone choosing to help. With your support, we can end the water crisis in our lifetime — and unlock a future where no child has to choose between water and school.
